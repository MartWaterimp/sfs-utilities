About

Placidity's SFS Utilities is an unofficial collection of browser tools for Spaceflight Simulator. The goal is to help users with their Spaceflight Simulator builds with browser tools.

cookie

Cookie-based saves

Custom fuels from the calculator are stored in browser cookies so your ratios and dry mass fractions come back the next time you open the page.
